Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our courses and admission process.

Which course is right for me?
The right course depends on your current class and target exam. For Classes 8–10, our Foundation courses are ideal. For engineering aspirants, choose JEE batches, and for medical, choose NEET batches. We recommend booking a free counseling session for personalized advice.
Is study material included in the course fee?
Yes, comprehensive and meticulously designed study material, including practice sheets, question banks, and test papers, is included in the course fee for all our programs.
Are there online batches available?
Yes, we offer both online and offline batches to suit your convenience and location.
